id="post_micro_134790190827" ><div class="post_content"><div class="post_content_inner"><p>Thinking in the car on the way to work. &nbsp;Maybe I don&rsquo;t trust Aleysha as much as I thought, which is why I feel so insecure. &nbsp;I think the breakup disrupted my trust. &nbsp;I was 110% sure she would hold on to us and never be involved with someone else. &nbsp;So when I found out that she had been with someone else, I was shattered. &nbsp;I was blinded by how much I wanted things to go back to the way they were, I failed to notice how my faith had permeated. &nbsp;And it isn&rsquo;t that I don&rsquo;t trust her, it&rsquo;s just the level of trust I has weakened. &nbsp;It&rsquo;s something that we need to build back up. &nbsp;</p><p>And it is apparent to me now that we have different metrics for sex. &nbsp;It&rsquo;s always been something I shared with the person I love. &nbsp;That&rsquo;s why I&rsquo;ve only been with Aleysha. &nbsp;To me, sex is the ultimate level of intimacy between two people and for her to share it with someone else hurt. &nbsp;It made me doubt what we had spent the last four years building. &nbsp;</p><p>And I understand we were broken up. &nbsp;And I know we were fixing our individualities, but I always had you in the back of my mind, as an end goal. &nbsp;And that&rsquo;s not to say you didn&rsquo;t, maybe you did, but you also admitted that you thought I was over you or that I wanted to just be friends or whatever. &nbsp;</p><p>And another issue is that things never added up for me. &nbsp;You started RA training while I was in Florida. &nbsp;I left August 14. &nbsp;I called you at least 3 times that week, if not more. &nbsp;Sometimes I was drunk, sometimes I wasn&rsquo;t, but I thought it proved you were on my mind. &nbsp;And I can&rsquo;t help but think how naiive or heartbroken you must&rsquo;ve been to think otherwise, or how heartbroken you must have been to reject me. &nbsp;I don&rsquo;t remember if I saw you between Florida and school, I think so though. &nbsp;Even if we didn&rsquo;t that means that two weeks had gone by without speaking, because I know we spoke on September 1. &nbsp;Everything seemed okay, we were visiting each other, I also helped your brother move in, which means I definitely saw you before September 1. &nbsp;So I don&rsquo;t understand how you weren&rsquo;t thinking of me. &nbsp;This all means in the two weeks we may not have spoken, between August 14 and whenever I helped you and your brother move in, events happened, events could have happened even after. &nbsp;And the truth is that I don&rsquo;t care about what Aleysha did. &nbsp;It&rsquo;s more about her saying that we didn&rsquo;t speak that often, because I know even in not trying, I was trying. &nbsp;</p><p>But I&rsquo;m glad that I finally realized the root of my insecurities, and finally got all of this out. &nbsp;It now allows me to tackle them head on. &nbsp;Maybe I was too afraid to admit that my faith in Aleysha had weakened, but I realize it&rsquo;s okay now. &nbsp;I think I felt like shit too because I thought all of it was my fault, and I realize now it isn&rsquo;t. &nbsp;</p><p>&nbsp;I&rsquo;m not sure how this will make Aleysha feel. &nbsp;I&rsquo;m not sure if I should tell her to read it now or later. &nbsp;I don&rsquo;t want to negatively impact her day. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p>I actually feel extremely relieved and I&rsquo;m ready to move forward. &nbsp;My head is so clear right now, it&rsquo;s pretty cool. &nbsp;Steysha for the win. &nbsp;We gonna make it. &nbsp;We&rsquo;re strong as
