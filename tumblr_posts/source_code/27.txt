<div class="post post_micro is_regular post_tumblelog_c8af769bb4f36da8f7ff143ed53dd6a5 not_mine is_original with_permalink no_source generic_source no_notes reblog_ui_refresh has_hover" id="post_micro_35977136404" ><div class="post_content"><div class="post_content_inner"><p>Maybe, just maybe, everything is this world does happen for a reason. &nbsp;Maybe everything is &ldquo;interconnected&rdquo; with one another. &nbsp;Maybe it isn&rsquo;t. &nbsp;I don&rsquo;t know what to believe anymore. &nbsp;Nature and the way things work is the single most complex story ever written. I have lived eighteen years. &nbsp; I have experienced so little, yet so much. I haven&rsquo;t traveled outside of North America. &nbsp;I haven&rsquo;t jumped out of a plane. &nbsp;I haven&rsquo;t finished college. &nbsp;I&rsquo;ve experienced eighteen years of living. &nbsp;I have experienced family, along with the good and the bad. &nbsp;I have experienced playing baseball for twp years in high school. &nbsp;I have experienced a semester of college. &nbsp;I have experienced love. &nbsp;I am experiencing love. &nbsp;I&rsquo;ve loved two girls in my life. &nbsp;Both of them began as infatuations. &nbsp;My attraction to them at the beginning is&nbsp;indescribable. &nbsp;Neither of them thought of me as weird, maybe a bit awkward. &nbsp;They seemed to not only accept me, like my parents, but they understood me. &nbsp;Neither of them ran for the hills, but towards me. &nbsp;They broke the barrier which&nbsp;separated my intrinsic character from the Steven Vazquez that my &ldquo;friends&rdquo;, teachers, and family witness. &nbsp;Kayla and Aleysha are the only two girls that I&rsquo;ve loved. &nbsp;</p>
<p>I loved them both, but I know that without a doubt that Aleysha is the only one that I&rsquo;ve ever been in love with. &nbsp;My heart beats for her. &nbsp;Other than my parents, she&rsquo;s the only one that I can trust with not only my life, but my love. &nbsp;I told her the other day that she makes me feel things that no other could, and I mean that. I&rsquo;ve revealed to her things that no one else could ever possibly know.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Yea she pisses me off, but it happens. &nbsp;I&rsquo;m sure I piss her off too. &nbsp;I never intend to upset her; I live to please her. &nbsp;I doubt she intends to upset me either. &nbsp;I know she loves me ALMOST as much as I love her. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Watching her sleep at 4:31 a.m. on November 18,2012. &nbsp;I know I&rsquo;m probably creepy, but she&rsquo;s so beautiful inside and out. &nbsp;She&rsquo;s able to view things much more for what they are. &nbsp;</p>
<p>I felt like a bitch the other day. &nbsp;I was getting emotional and ish. &nbsp;We were discussing how I was making her chose between her mom and me. &nbsp;I felt absolutely horrible. &nbsp;I would never want to make her do that. &nbsp;What I want is her to not sit and be attacked by her deranged mother. &nbsp;I want to defend her. &nbsp;I want to take care of her. &nbsp;She deserves everything she wants and more. &nbsp;She doesn&rsquo;t realize how lonely and envious I get at home either. &nbsp;I try my best not to complain about anything. &nbsp;Whether is be little things like my hunger, or big things like pain. &nbsp;I don&rsquo;t want to burden her, or anyone else for that matter. &nbsp;She may get lonely, but she has roommates and this whole other world. &nbsp;It&rsquo;s a great new experience. &nbsp;My routine has not changed from high school. &nbsp;I don&rsquo;t have the opportunity to hang out in a dorm and order food and ish. &nbsp;I hang out in my room and eat whatever is home. &nbsp;When I was turned away and shaking and feeling like shit the other day, you don&rsquo;t know how badly I wanted to tell you that I don&rsquo;t have friends, and that you&rsquo;re my only really friend. &nbsp;You&rsquo;re the only one I care about. &nbsp;Everyone thinks I&rsquo;m strange. &nbsp;I&rsquo;m just not a very sociable person anymore, part of that is because I don&rsquo;t enjoy bullshit, and it seems that is what most people do. &nbsp;</p>
<p>I would be extremely disappointed if you didn&rsquo;t get to go to Manhattan College, but I would understand. &nbsp;I just want you to be happy. &nbsp;You just woke up and began to speak to me. &nbsp;I love you so much. &nbsp;You are my best friend. &nbsp;My life. &nbsp;My world. STEYSHA. &nbsp;Goodnight.</p></div></div><div class="post_glass post_micro_glass" title=""><a target="_blank" class="hover" title="" href="http://seniorscooba.tumblr.com/post/35977136404/maybe-just-maybe-everything-is-this-world-does"><div class="hover_inner"><span class="post_date">Nov 18, 2012</span></div>
